Chequing account CDIC coverage

The balance in your chequing account is held in trust for you with members of the Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation (“CDIC”). CDIC protects eligible deposits held at CDIC member institutions in case of a member institution’s failure. Although Wealthsimple is not a CDIC member institution, under the trust framework, funds must be spread across up to 10 CDIC member institutions in order for up to $1 million in deposits to benefit from applicable CDIC coverage. Coverage is free and automatic.

FAQs

How do I become a Premium or Generation client?

You’ll automatically become a Premium client when you reach $100,000 with .

Similarly, you’ll automatically become a Generation client when you reach $500,000 with .

What counts towards the $100,000 or $500,000 minimums?

Your status is determined by whichever is higher — the value of your net deposits, or the value of your entire portfolio.

So whether you’re a long-time client whose portfolio value has gone up over time (nice), or a brand-new client transferring to us, we want you to have every opportunity to qualify for Premium or Generation.

What accounts are included in the $100,000 or $500,000 minimums?

Almost all your accounts are included! TFSAs, RESPs, RRSPs, GRSPs, LIRAs, and non-registered accounts — they’re all included, whether they’re Managed investing accounts or Self-directed accounts. So are your Crypto, Save, and chequing accounts.

What happens if my net deposits and my total assets go below $100,000 or $500,000?

If both your net deposits and total assets with fall and stay below the $100,000 or $500,000 minimum you will be subject to a change in status.

I pay for USD accounts. What happens when I reach Premium?

You’ll no longer be charged for USD accounts, but you’ll keep receiving the same great benefits. You’ll save $10 a month, and continue on your path to reaching Generation status.

What is a Medcan health plan?

Medcan's Comprehensive Health Plan helps you optimize your health now and in the future with a wide range of services. They include an annual health assessment, diagnostic testing with same-day results, on-demand appointments, travel health support, and genetics and genetic counselling.

For more information about the Medcan perks included with Premium, read here.

For more information about the Medcan perks included with Generation read here.

How does the free USD accounts trial work?

Once you sign up, you’ll receive the benefits of USD accounts for free. After the 30 day trial, you’ll automatically be billed $10/month for upcoming months.

To exchange CAD to USD, you’ll still be charged a 1.5% currency conversion fee, or you can transfer USD from another account for free.

*How can I access the 0.2% management fee?

Our management fees for Generation clients range from 0.4% for clients who have $500,000 with us, to 0.2% for clients who have $10,000,000 with us. To learn which rate you qualify for, reach out to our team of advisors here.
